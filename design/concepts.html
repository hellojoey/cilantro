Adaptive color

Herb green is home base, but the whole scene re-tints to the question's vibe — society goes slate blue, intimacy goes plum. Answer the demo twice and watch it shift.

Fine Print 2.0

A toggle beneath the card — collapsed by default. Open it for details, cited perspectives (placeholders here; real articles come from the research pipeline with your approval), topic hashtags, and tappable thumbnails of the gardens this question connects to.

Motion

Each shape wanders on its own unrepeating path — a slow random drift to a nearby spot, an easing pause, then a new direction. Same gentle amplitude as before, but organic instead of metronomic. Calming, never busy.
